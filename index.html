Our Philosophy

“TENACE comes from the Latin tenax — persistent, unyielding. Real progress isn't about intensity or motivation. It's about showing up, session after session.”

See it in action

Built for how you actually train

Workout Logging

We handle the logging.
You handle the lifting.

Log sets, reps, and weights in seconds. The built-in rest timer starts automatically between sets — so you never lose your pace or forget where you left off. Three tracking modes cover every type of exercise in your program.

Strength Progress

Every rep you add is recorded forever.

See your strength curve across every exercise, from your first session to your latest. The progress you can't see day-to-day becomes undeniable month-to-month. Personal records are detected automatically and logged quietly — no fanfare required.

Body Tracking

Train consistently.
The body follows.

Log body weight, body fat percentage, and circumferences over time. Sparkline charts show the long arc of your progress — not just today's number. Because the scale alone has never told the whole story.

Everything your training needs

Weekly Planning

Assign plans to each day of the week. Open the app and your workout is there, ready to go. No searching, no deciding.

Achievement Badges

Milestone badges that celebrate consistency over time. Earn them for streaks, session counts, and long-term commitment.

Full Training History

Every session, every set — always with you. Look back at how far you've come and fuel the next session.
