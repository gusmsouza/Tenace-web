Our Philosophy

“TENACE comes from the Latin tenax — persistent, unyielding. Real progress isn't about intensity or motivation. It's about showing up, session after session.”

What's Inside

Everything your training needs

Smart Workout Logging

Three tracking modes: weight & reps, reps only, and timed sets. Covers every exercise in your program — from heavy compound lifts to bodyweight circuits and long-hold planks.

Progressive Overload Hints

The app detects when you're consistently hitting your target and suggests a conservative weight increase. Science-backed, never aggressive — because slow progress is the progress that sticks.

Achievement Badges

9 milestone badges that celebrate your journey. Earn them again and again — there's no finish line in TENACE. Every session counts, and every streak deserves recognition.

Body Composition Tracking

Log body weight, body fat percentage, and circumferences. Sparkline charts show your trend over time so you can see the arc of your progress — not just today's number.

Weekly Planning

Assign training plans to each day of the week. The app shows today's workout the moment you open it — no searching, no deciding. Just show up and train.

Training History

Every session, every set — expandable and always with you. Your entire training life in one place, so you can look back at how far you've come and fuel the next session.
